Oil

Various oil prices from two oil providers: Oil WTI and Oil Brent.

Oil WTI - West Texas Intermediate, also known as Texas light sweet. This is a grade of crude oil used as a benchmark in oil pricing. This oil is described as light crude oil because of its relatively low density, and sweet because of it's low sulfur content.


Oil Brent - Brent Crude is a major trading classification of sweet light crude oil which serves as another benchmark in oil pricing. This oil is described as light crude oil because of it's low density, and sweet because of it's low sulfur content.


Whats the difference?

The major difference between the crude oils is ...

THE LOCATION!

West Texas Intermediate(WTI) is sourced from U.S. oil fields found in Texas, Louisiana, and North Dakota.

Brent crude oil is sourced from oil fields in the North Sea between the Shetland Islands and Norway
